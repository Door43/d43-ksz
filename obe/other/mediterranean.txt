====== Mediterranean, Mediterranean Sea, the Great Sea ======

===== Facts: =====

A large sea that borders Israel to it's East, Turkey and Italy to it's North, Africa to it's South and Spain to it's West.

  * The Great Sea includes the Aegean, Adriatic, Ionian and the Levant seas.
  * To the Hebrews the Great Sea lay behind them when they faced East, so it was sometimes referred to as the Western Sea.
  * The Mediterranean or Great Sea made it easy for trade and travel in ancient times since it bordered so many countries.
