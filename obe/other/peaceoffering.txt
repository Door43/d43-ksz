====== peace offering ======

===== Facts: =====

An Old Testament offering to God through a priest for the fellowship between God and the worshiper.

  * Leviticus Chapters 3 and 7 describe the peace offering.
  * The offering  would be animal flesh, bread, or both.
  * The peace offering could include thankfulness to God.
